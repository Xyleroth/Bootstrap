Our Mission

The mission of the Bureau of Epidemiology is to prevent sickness and death from infectious diseases and environmental hazards, and monitor diseases to reduce spread. The Bureau is also responsible for monitoring and responding to potential bioterrorism threats/events, communicable disease outbreaks, epidemics, and other unusual occurrences of illness.
